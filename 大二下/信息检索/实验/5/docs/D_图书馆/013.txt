英文版视频指南——论文收录引用证明English guide videos for inclusion and citation report
图书馆　2023/12/19 9:37:00
 
为本校师生出具论文收录引用证明是图书馆的一项“热门”服务，依据委托人提供的信息，查询文献被Web of Science（SCI、SSCI、A&HCI、CPCI-S）、EI、CSCD、CSSCI数据库收录或引用的情况，以及JCR/中科院期刊影响因子和期刊分区、ESI高被引论文等。
Inclusion and citation report for campus teachers and students is a popular service of the library. Based on the information provided by the applicant, we will verify whether the document has been included or cited in Web of Science (SCI, SSCI, A&HCI, CPCI), EI, CSCD and CSSCI, as well as the impact factor and journal partition of JCR/CSCI journals, ESI highly cited papers and so on.
此前，图书馆已精心准备了一系列中文版操作视频，为大家提供指引与便利。为了进一步方便外籍教师与留学生读者们使用论文收录引用证明服务，我们特意在中文版指南的基础之上，制作了英文版指引视频，帮助大家解决在申请过程中遇到的各类问题，快快收藏起来吧！
Previously, the Library has prepared a series of guide videos in Chinese version to provide the readers with guidance and convenience. In order to further facilitate the use of inclusion and citation report service by foreign teachers and students, we specially make the English guide videos based on the Chinese version. These videos will assist you in resolving various issues that may arise throughout the application process, so don't hesitate to bookmark them!
1．怎样为自己委托How to apply for yourself?
2．怎样为校内其他用户委托How to apply for other campus users?
3．怎样为校外用户委托How to apply for Off-campus users?
4．怎样进行团队委托How to apply for a team?
5．文献导入方法Document import methods.
视频链接：http://info.lib.szu.edu.cn/cn/archives/3855
