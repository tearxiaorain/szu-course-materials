国际交流与合作部关于举办新引进外籍教职员工培训的通知Orientation Agenda for New Foreign Faculty Members of Shenzhen University
国际交流与合作部　2023/11/9 9:32:00
  关于举办新引进外籍教职工培训的通知
Notice on Orientation Agenda for New Foreign Faculty Members of Shenzhen University 2023
    为便利外籍教职员工来校工作生活，学校拟于2023年11月25日（星期六）举办新进外籍教职工培训。现将有关事项通知如下：
In order to facilitate the new foreign staff to better orient, the University plans to hold an orientation workshop for new foreign staff on November 25, 2023 (Saturday). The relevant matters are hereby notified as follows:
一、培训对象
2022年1月1日至2023年10月31日期间入职的外籍教职工（包含博士后，专职研究员等各类外籍教职工，具体名单见附件）。
I. Target Participants
Foreign faculty and staff recruited from January 1, 2022 to October 31, 2023 (including postdoctoral fellows, full-time researchers and other types of foreign faculty and staff, see the annex for the detailed list).
二、培训内容
外籍培训内容：校史校情校规、国情法律法规、外汇知识（具体培训日程安排见附件）。
II. Workshop Topics
Topics: SZU in A Nutshell, Legal Regulations and Foreign Currency and Banking. (See the copy of Agenda attached).
