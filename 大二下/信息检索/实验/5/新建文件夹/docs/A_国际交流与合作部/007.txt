国际交流与合作部关于举办新引进外籍教职员工培训的通知 Orientation Agenda for New Foreign Faculty Members of Shenzhen University
国际交流与合作部　2024/12/17 17:07:00
         为便利外籍教职员工来校工作生活，学校拟于2024年12月20日（星期五）举办新进外籍教职工培训。现将有关事项通知如下：
     In order to facilitate the new foreign staff to better orient, the University plans to hold an orientation workshop for new foreign staff on December 20, 2024 (Friday). The relevant matters are hereby notified as follows:
一、培训对象
2023年11月1日至2024年12月期间入职的外籍教职工（包含博士后，专职研究员等各类外籍教职工）。
I. Target Participants
Foreign faculty and staff recruited from November 1, 2023 to December, 2024 (including postdoctoral fellows, full-time researchers and other types of foreign faculty and staff).
二、培训内容
外籍培训内容：校史校情校规、国情法律法规（具体培训日程安排见附件）。
II. Workshop Topics
Topics: SZU Introduction, Legal Regulations (See attachment).
