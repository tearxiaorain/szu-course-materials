东京大学工学研究院暑期教育项目（ESEP-G2025）招生通知（2025项目内容已更新）
国际交流与合作部　2024/9/29 17:15:00
 
东京大学工学研究院（Scholl of Engineering, The University of Tokyo)与我校于2024年4月缔结学术合作协议。东京大学工学研究院覆盖的工学类学科非常广泛，包括物理、化学、环境工程、土木、建筑、信息等学科专业，几乎涵盖了我校工科类的所有学科。（可点击以下链接了解协议院校相关内容）
http://dir.u-tokyo.ac.jp/SysKyotei/01/kyoutei/search

ESEP为本科生和研究生提供在东京大学顶尖实验室参与科研项目的机会，仅面向合作高校的在校生。参与者将在实验室进行为期六周的研究，每周五天、每天约七小时。此外，项目还包括一天的巴士游览和日本文化体验。
项目日期：2025年6月24日至8月1日
申请条件：必须为合作高校的全日制学生，本科生需完成至少两年的学业(报名时）。
参与人数：最多25名学生(东京大学的全球合作院校）
项目费用：免费
奖学金：满足特定条件的参与者可获得每月80,000日元的奖学金。

更多详情，请参考以下相关链接和内容。
http://oice.t.u-tokyo.ac.jp/exchange/Summerprogram.html

ESEP-G2025
1.　Program period
June 24th – August 1st, 2025
2.　Eligibility
Applicants must:
be full-time students (including graduated students) enrolled at one of our *partner universities and not have graduated by the end of our program.
have completed at least two years of undergraduate study before the start of the program
*Partner universities : (List of partner universities for ESEP-G 2025)
3.　Number of participants
Up to 25 students
4.　Program fee
Free
5.　Host labs and research topics
List of Host Laboratories (ESEP-G 2025)
Students will be hosted by one of the laboratories. Any special academic requirements for training in each lab are also indicated on the list.
6.　Program schedule
June 24:　Orientation & campus tour
June 25 to July 31:　Research activities(full-time)
TBD:　Final presentation
August 1:　Report submission
 ≪Activities≫
TBD:　Japanese lesson(2 hours)
TBD:　One-day bus tour
TBD:　Japanese drum workshop(half day)
7.　Requirements
Applicants must:
l participate in the entire duration of the program
l have high rated academic performance and a high level of English proficiency
l have a strong interest in pursuing a Ph. D
l remain enrolled at their current home university during the program period. When an applicant is in the last year of their degree program, the graduation must be taken place after the program ends
l not have joined this program before
l secure overseas travel insurance in their home countries before coming to Japan, and bear the premium of personal liability insurance (see "Insurance" for details)
l refrain from attending other courses and being employed
l respect the work of all laboratory members and participate in activities in the laboratory
l able to conduct research activities on their own initiative
l have a cooperative attitude and can live together with other students in a shared house
 8.　Financial support
Scholarship : Based on the ESEP Scholarship guideline of the School of Engineering, The University of Tokyo, JPY 160,000 (JPY 80,000 monthly × 2 months)will be provided to participants who meet the following requirements.The scholarship will be paid on a monthly basis in cash.
participating in the entire duration of the program
staying in the accommodation provided by the program
attending all required activities and social events
submitting a final report to the School of Engineering at the end of the program
 NOTE: Participants must bear the following costs:
l Air ticket (round-trip ticket)
l Overseas travel insurance and personal liability insurance
l Visa fee, domestic transportation fee
l Daily commuting expenses for coming to campus from the accommodation
l Everyday living expenses
 9.　Insurance
Selected students must:
pay a liability insurance premium specific to the research activities on the first day of the program. This insurance will cover any unintentional accidents involving another person and/or laboratory equipment during research activities. The premium will cost about JPY 5,000 per person.
be covered by their own travel insurance for the entire duration of their stay in Japan and must submit copies of their insurance policy to us.
 10.　Accommodation
Designated accommodations will be provided free of charge to students during of the program. The details will be announced after the selection process.
 How to apply
Application period : November 15th, 2024 - January 15th, 2025, 3:00PM (Japan time)
ESEP-G online application system
https://application.t.u-tokyo.ac.jp/esep-g/
Register your information in the online application system and upload the required documents to the “My Page”. You can choose up to a second choice of laboratory from List of Host Laboratories (ESEP-G 2025)
Required documents to upload
Photo : Taken within the last 6 months
Personal statement (designated form for ESEP-G)
Official certificate of current enrollment
Students enrolled in their final year of the current university must submit one that includes the expected graduation date.
Official Undergraduate academic transcript in English (both undergraduate students and graduate students)
Official Graduate academic transcript in English (graduate students only)
English test scores report (IELTS/TOEIC/TOEFL/ other external tests)
Exemption: those whose nationality is USA, UK, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, Ireland, India or currently enrolled at a university in those countries.
Copy of Passport: Photocopy of the photo page of your valid passport. If you do not have a passport, upload a copy of your official ID.
Letter of recommendation (suggested format)
The letter must be issued by applicant's supervising faculty member.Please refer to the "How to upload recommendation letter".
NOTE:
* Please submit the most recent certificate of enrollment and academic transcript.
* As some universities will enter holiday periods, make sure to prepare the required documents well in advance.

Application deadline : January 15th, 2025, 3:00 PM (Japan time)
Please submit all documents by the deadline. Incomplete documents may result in disqualification from the selection process.
Selection
Candidates will be selected through document screening. Also, some laboratories conduct online interviews in early February.
* Make sure to provide an email address on our online application system where you can be contacted.
Selection results : Middle of March 2025
The selection results will be notified by email.
